# What happens in the first few sessions?


## Before you arrive

Nothing to prepare. You do not need to have your story organized, your childhood mapped, or a diagnosis in mind. See it as a time set aside for you to share whatever it is that you wish for me to understand about you in the moment.

## The first ten minutes

We handle the logistics, such as confidentiality and what it covers, how scheduling works.

## The rest of the hour

Mostly, I listen. I'll ask questions like:

- What made now the moment you reached out?
- What do you wish for me to know about you?

At the end, we talk about fit. Not every therapist is right for every person, and a good first session includes the freedom to say "this isn't it." If I think someone else would serve you better, I'll help you find them.
